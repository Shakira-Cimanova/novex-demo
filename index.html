Why Novex

Every AI tool gives you answers. Novex gives you answers you can trust — with the source trail to prove it. Built for professionals who can't afford to be wrong.

Claim-Level Citation Tagging
Every factual sentence is marked Cited, Inference, or Assumption — so you always know what's proven vs. reasoned.
Trustworthiness Score (T-Score)
Every report receives an algorithmic integrity rating across Citation Coverage, Source Credibility, Fabrication Risk, and Data Freshness.
Live Web Research
Novex doesn't rely on static training data. It searches the live web, triages sources by credibility tier, and checks freshness in real time.
Domain-Aware Guardrails
Finance, medical, legal, and safety-sensitive topics trigger specialized policies — neutral language, freshness warnings, and professional consultation prompts.
Provenance Ledger
